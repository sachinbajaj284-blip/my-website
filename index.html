How We Work

The Lume Care Approach

A structured, three-step journey — built on science, delivered with warmth — so you never have to navigate the future alone.


Trusted by families across Haryana, Delhi NCR, and now PAN India — because good counselling changes everything.

01

Step One

Scientific Discovery

We begin not with assumptions, but with answers. Using internationally validated psychometric tools — aptitude tests, interest inventories, and personality assessments — we build a comprehensive, data-driven picture of your child's unique mind. This isn't guesswork; it's a scientific foundation. Every recommendation we make is rooted in this deep understanding of who your child truly is — not who the marks say they should be.

02

Step Two

Expert 1:1 Counselling

Numbers alone don't tell a story — a trained counsellor does. In our personalised one-on-one sessions, we connect your child's psychometric data to real-world careers, industry realities, and honest conversations about aspirations and fears. We bridge the gap between what the data reveals and what the world offers, guiding both students and parents through every question, concern, and hesitation with patience and clinical expertise.

03

Step Three

Your Confident Future

Every student leaves Lume Live with something tangible: a personalised Career & Life Roadmap — a clear, actionable plan for the next 3 to 5 years. From stream selection to entrance exams, from first jobs to higher education, this document becomes your family's north star. No more second-guessing at 11 PM. No more "what if." Just a structured, hopeful path forward — chosen with clarity, not fear.

About the Founder

Where Academic Rigour Meets Human Empathy

Lume Live was born from a deeply personal conviction: that every student in India — regardless of their city, school, or family background — deserves access to world-class, science-backed counselling. Not advice pulled from the air. Not generational assumptions. Real guidance, grounded in psychology.

The founder holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Guidance and Counselling (PGDCGC) from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi — one of India's most respected institutions for psychological sciences. This rigorous academic training in clinical assessment, psychometric methodology, and therapeutic counselling creates a rare dual competency: the technical precision to interpret aptitude data, and the emotional intelligence to meet a distressed student exactly where they are.

"I've seen too many brilliant students go down the wrong path — not because they lacked potential, but because nobody gave them the right mirror at the right time. Lume Live is that mirror."

Proudly rooted in Rohtak, Haryana, and serving students across India online — because geography should never limit a young person's future.
