Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our counselling process, sessions, and how we can help you or your child.

What is career counselling and who needs it?
Career counselling is a structured process that helps individuals understand their strengths, interests, and personality to make informed career decisions. It is beneficial for students in Class 9–12 who are choosing streams, college students unsure about their path, and even working professionals considering a career change. At Lume Live, we use psychometric assessments combined with 1:1 expert guidance.
How does online counselling work? Is it as effective as in-person?
Yes, online counselling is highly effective. All sessions are conducted via secure video calls (Google Meet or Zoom) from the comfort of your home. Research shows that tele-counselling delivers outcomes comparable to in-person sessions. We have successfully guided 500+ students and professionals across India through online sessions.
What happens in the first ₹499 wellness session?
Your first session is a 45-minute 1:1 conversation with Sachin sir. We begin by understanding your current concerns — whether it is stream confusion, career anxiety, or emotional stress. Based on your needs, we may conduct a brief psychometric screening and provide immediate actionable insights. You will leave with clarity on your next steps.
Is everything I share kept confidential?
Absolutely. 100% confidentiality is our highest priority. Everything shared during sessions — personal struggles, family issues, academic concerns — stays strictly between you and your counsellor. We follow professional ethical guidelines and do not share any information with parents, schools, or third parties without your explicit consent.
What is a psychometric test and do I need one?
A psychometric test is a scientifically validated assessment that measures your aptitude, interests, and personality traits. It removes guesswork from career decisions by matching your natural strengths to suitable career paths. We recommend it for all students in Class 10+ and anyone facing career confusion. The test takes 30–45 minutes and results are explained in your session.
Can parents attend the session with their child?
Yes, we offer joint parent-child sessions upon request. These are especially helpful when there is disagreement about stream or career choices. Our approach ensures the student's voice is heard while addressing parent concerns with data-driven insights. We also offer separate parent guidance sessions to help you support your child effectively.
How many sessions will I need?
Most students achieve significant clarity in 2–3 sessions. The first session focuses on assessment and immediate guidance. Follow-up sessions deepen the roadmap, address emotional blocks, and refine action plans. For emotional wellness concerns, the number varies based on individual needs — we never push unnecessary sessions.
Do you conduct sessions for schools and groups?
Yes! We regularly conduct career awareness workshops, mental health seminars, and psychometric assessment drives for schools across Haryana and beyond. We have impacted 500+ students through school sessions. Schools can partner with us for half-day workshops, full-day career fairs, or ongoing counselling support.
