Our Method

The Lume Care Approach

Our three-step process isn't just a consultation — it's a scientific, structured journey from confusion to clarity. Every step is designed to give parents and students the certainty they deserve.


01
Scientific Discovery

Understanding Who You Truly Are

Before any advice is given, we listen — deeply. Through validated psychometric assessments and structured discovery sessions, we build a precise picture of your child's aptitude, personality, interests, and values. This isn't a quiz from a website. It's the same scientific rigour used by career specialists in top institutions — now available right here in Rohtak and online across India.

02
Expert 1:1 Counselling

Connecting Data to Real Opportunity

Numbers alone don't build futures — context does. In our expert one-to-one sessions, our counsellor maps your assessment results to real-world careers, sectors, and pathways. We ask the hard questions that school teachers and relatives can't: What drives you? What are you willing to work for? Where do you see yourself in a decade? The answers shape a strategy, not just a suggestion.

03
Confident Future

Your Personal Roadmap — In Writing

You leave every Lume Live engagement with a tangible, written roadmap: specific career options ranked by fit, recommended subjects and colleges, next steps for the next 6–12 months, and a clear action plan. No more late-night worrying about 'what if'. Just a structured, confident path forward — crafted just for your child, by a qualified professional who genuinely cares.

Common Questions

Questions Parents Ask Us

Is the free demo session really free?+

Yes, absolutely. The 30-minute demo session is completely free with no obligation. It's our way of letting you experience the Lume Care Approach before making any commitment. We believe you'll see the value — but the choice is entirely yours.

My child is in Class 9. Is it too early?+

Not at all — in fact, Class 9 is an ideal time to begin career exploration. Early awareness reduces the pressure of Class 10 decisions significantly. We tailor our sessions to the student's age and maturity, making the process comfortable and age-appropriate.

Do you offer online sessions for students outside Rohtak?+

Yes! Lume Live is fully equipped for online sessions via video call. We work with students across Haryana and all of India. Our psychometric assessments are also conducted digitally, so geography is never a barrier to quality guidance.

How is this different from a school counsellor?+

School counsellors serve hundreds of students and often focus primarily on academic issues. At Lume Live, every student receives dedicated 1:1 attention, validated psychometric tools, a written personalised roadmap, and follow-up support — this is professional, structured counselling, not a 15-minute chat.

Are the emotional wellness sessions confidential?+

Confidentiality is the foundation of all our wellness sessions. Everything shared remains strictly between the student and their counsellor. We follow professional ethical guidelines to ensure every client feels safe, heard, and respected.

What is the fee structure?+

We offer transparent, package-based pricing designed to be accessible. Please book a free demo session or WhatsApp us directly for current pricing. We also offer flexible payment options for comprehensive packages.
