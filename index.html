Our Process

The Lume Care Approach

A structured, three-step process that transforms anxiety into actionable direction. Built on science. Delivered with empathy.

1
Step One
Scientific Discovery

We begin with globally validated psychometric assessments that map your child's unique aptitudes, interests, personality type, and learning style. This isn't a quiz — it's a precision instrument used by career counsellors worldwide. The data tells us what no interview alone can: what your child is genuinely wired to do well, and what lights them up from the inside. Parents, for the first time, you'll have something concrete and scientific to anchor every conversation around careers.

2
Step Two
Expert 1:1 Counselling

The numbers mean nothing without context. In a personalised one-on-one session, our counsellor connects the psychometric data to the real world — exploring career options, higher education pathways, market realities, and your family's unique situation. This is where anxiety turns into clarity. We address the fears you haven't said out loud: "What if my child chooses wrong?" "What if they don't get into a good college?" We answer these — calmly, expertly, with evidence.

3
Step Three
The Confident Future

Every student leaves with a personalised written roadmap — a clear, step-by-step guide covering stream choice, career options, skill-building priorities, and a 12-month action plan. This isn't generic advice from the internet. It is a document built specifically for your child, by a qualified professional, grounded in their own data. Parents, this is the structured clarity you've been looking for. You'll walk away knowing the plan is in place — and in good hands.

Meet the Founder

A Counsellor Who Understands Both the Science and the Heart.

"I started Lume Care because I saw too many brilliant students — right here in Haryana — making life-altering decisions in the dark. Science can light the way."

The founder of Lume Care holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Guidance and Counselling (PGDCGC) from Jamia Millia Islamia — one of India's most respected central universities for the study of human psychology, behaviour, and counselling practice. This rigorous, academically intensive programme does something rare: it trains counsellors not just in the therapeutic dimensions of human emotion, but in the technical precision of psychometric assessment and vocational guidance.

What this means for your child is significant. When you sit across from our counsellor, you are working with someone who can simultaneously read a psychometric report with scientific accuracy and detect the quiet anxiety beneath a student's composed face. The emotional and the analytical are not separate disciplines here — they are seamlessly integrated.

Based in Rohtak, and deeply connected to the academic and social fabric of Haryana, the Lume Care founder is committed to a singular mission: bringing the kind of structured, world-class career guidance that major metro students take for granted — to every student across this region and beyond. Whether you are in Rohtak, Ambala, Panipat, or anywhere else in India, you deserve expert guidance — and that is exactly what we deliver.
